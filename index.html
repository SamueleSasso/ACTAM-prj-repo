Welcome ! — Quick Start Guide

Turn the KNOBS to set the STEPS, PULSES, and OFFSET of each sequence.

Click the DOTS in the visualizer to toggle the pulses as desired.

Use the VELOCITY panel to paint the dynamics of the selected sequences.

You can load a custom SAMPLE per track.

The MIDI button will export the selected tracks in a single file,
ready to load into your favorite DAW !

Press START to play!
